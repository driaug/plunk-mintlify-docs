You can use Zapier to send events and emails to your users.

## Getting Started
Accept the [Zapier Integration](https://zapier.com/developer/public-invite/164389/1298218be72fcd0b374be2dbce9f5599/) and create a new Zap.

The Zapier integrations supports two actions:
- `Send Event`
- `Send Email`
